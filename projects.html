Hotel Room Booking System

Undertook this project as part of my OOP classwork in 2018. Collaborated with a team of three classmates to design a program that books guests into a hotel depending on the floor they prefer.

Some of the skills gained in the process include: An appreciation for teamwork, the use of Git for version control, a good understanding of the Software Development Lifecycle, and the need to write well-designed and documented code


Jiji House

Completed this project while a mentee at KamiLimu as part of its Innovation curriculum. Collaborated with a team of two to identify a pressing socio-economic challenge facing the society, ideate a solution for it, and pitch it to a team of potential investors.

As many tenants face difficulties while looking for rental houses in Nairobi, the solution identified - Jiji House - was a web-based platform that used 3D cameras and geo-tags to enable easy visiting and viewing of available housing units.

Some of the skills gained in the process was how to identify real-world problems worth solving, the use of design thinking in coming up with solutions, creating an effective pitch deck, and pitching to investors
